Computer Science
Awaybot
(Fall 2016) As the capstone project for his graduate degree in data science from UC Berkeley, Alex worked with a team to create Awaybot. Awaybot is a new way of organizing information on Slack. Awaybot listens to to conversations in real time as they unfold. It uses natural language processing algorithms to divide the conversation into topics and summarize these topics. When the user requests, Awaybot returns a summary of the conversations that the user missed.
