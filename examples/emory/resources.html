Computer Science Programs
There is a national push for all students to learn computer science and become proficient programmers, and this is especially important for students who are interested in mathematics. Many mathematicians do some programming in the course of their research, and writing code develops logical and creative thinking skills. There are a ton of apps, websites, computer games, and even board games that teach programming!Start Code offers classes and summer coding camps in Toco Hills and Decatur.
