Directions and Parking Information
Emory's Math and Science Center is located at 400 Dowman Dr. Parking is available at Emory's Oxford Road Deck, located at 1390 Oxford Rd behind the Barnes and Noble. Although you will have to take a ticket, parking there is free on weekends (and evenings after 7 pm). Here's a map, including a few backup parking spots. Navigating on college campuses can be a bit confusing, so please take a look at the map and leave yourself a few extra minutes if you have not been here before.
